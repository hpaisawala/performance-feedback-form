Performance Rating Definitions (Reference Guide)

1: Unsatisfactory Consistently fails to meet basic role requirements. Immediate intervention is needed.
2: Needs Improvement Performance is inconsistent. Meets some objectives but lacks quality in others.
3: Meets Expectations A solid performer. Consistently achieves targets and demonstrates required skills.
4: Exceeds Expectations Regularly goes above and beyond. Proactive and delivers results above standard.
5: Outstanding Exceptional performance. Has a transformative impact on the team or projects.
